About Us

Our mission at ScanShape UG (haftungsbeschränkt) is to redefine commerce and elevate it to a forward-thinking digital level. We provide a virtual reality application that serves as an innovative platform for sellers, offering them a comprehensive virtual environment. In this environment, sellers can capture, edit, and directly offer 3D models of products – particularly furniture – for sale online using modern smartphone technology. At the same time, we emphasize sustainability and user-friendliness, ensuring that both experienced retailers and newcomers to e-commerce benefit from our advanced features.

Description of the Product

Our virtual reality platform combines state-of-the-art 3D scanning technology with an interactive user interface to create a completely new shopping experience. Sellers have the opportunity to capture, edit, and present their products in realistic 3D models using a smartphone within a digital environment. Buyers can scan their own spaces and virtually furnish them with the offered products, gaining a realistic impression of how these items will look in their home. This integration of augmented reality, e-commerce, and artificial intelligence elevates the shopping experience to a new level.

For Users

We have developed a unique shopping experience that goes far beyond traditional online shops. With the ScanShape UG (haftungsbeschränkt) app, you can easily scan your own living space using your smartphone and transfer it into a virtual environment. You then have the opportunity to choose from a wide variety of 3D furniture models and place them in your digitally created room.

This allows you to experience firsthand, even before purchasing, how the selected furniture fits into your home, the impact it creates in your personal space, and how it appears under different lighting conditions. This immersive experience enables you to test design ideas, try out various combinations, and ultimately make an informed purchase decision.

Our user-friendly interface guides you step by step through the entire process – from room measurement to final product placement – and also provides detailed product information, customer reviews, and interactive features that personalize your shopping experience. By seamlessly integrating virtual reality into the purchasing process, we reduce uncertainties, minimize mis-purchases, and create an engaging, forward-thinking shopping experience.
