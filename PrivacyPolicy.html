Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024-05-29

1. Information We Collect

1.1. For All Users

When you interact with our ScanShape platform, we automatically log certain data from HTTP requests. This information includes:

  • Timestamp: The time at which the request was made.
  • IP Address: Used for geolocation and abuse prevention.
  • Page URL: The address of the requested page.
  • Platform & Operating System: Details of your device environment.
  • Preferred Language/Locale: Defaults to your browser’s locale, modifiable by you.
  • Download Tracking: Whether an IP address has downloaded a specific package within the last 14 days.

1.2. With an Account

If you register for an account, additional information is collected, including:

  • Email Address: Used for communications and account recovery.
  • Passwords: Securely hashed and salted using BCrypt.
  • Profile Information: Such as website URLs and donation links.
  • User-Generated Content: Comments, forum threads, reviews, etc.
  • Audit Log: Records of significant actions (e.g., logins, edits) with timestamps.
  • Forum Usernames: Collected to index forum topics and maintain unique identification.

Note: Packages (releases, screenshots, and metadata) are not considered personal information. Please refrain from providing additional personal details beyond what is required.

2. How We Use the Collected Information

  • Object Scanning Data: Our mobile app uses the rear camera to scan objects and locations. This data is stored only on your device for design and measurement purposes and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • HTTP Request Logs: Used for debugging, abuse prevention, and generating aggregated usage statistics (e.g., package download counts).
  • Email Communications: To send essential system messages, password resets, privacy policy updates, and notifications (which you can configure or opt out of).
  • User Authentication: Passwords are used solely to authenticate your access to the platform.
  • Audit Logging: Records actions that may affect the security or integrity of the platform for troubleshooting and security analysis.
  • Locale Settings: Helps customize the interface and translate communications into your preferred language.
  • IP Address Monitoring: IP addresses are logged to monitor and combat abuse and are cached for 14 days to avoid duplicate download counts.

TrueDepth API Usage: Our app utilizes the TrueDepth API to enhance the accuracy of object scanning. No facial or personally identifiable facial data is collected, stored, or processed by our app.

3. Data Sharing and Storage

Third Parties: We do not share personal information with third parties, except for necessary error logging and performance monitoring (via Sentry.io) using anonymized data.

Data Storage: Our production servers are located in Germany. Encrypted backups are stored in the United Kingdom and, for redundancy, may also be stored in other jurisdictions (e.g., US or EU). By using our service, you consent to the transfer and storage of data within these regions.

4. Data Access and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • HTTP request logs are accessible only by the admin for debugging and abuse prevention purposes. These logs may be shared with select personnel after removing personal information.
  • Encrypted backups are accessible only to authorized staff (e.g., moderators, core developers) under a dual-control system.
  • Email addresses are visible only to moderators and admins for support purposes and are not shared externally.
  • Passwords are stored securely using strong hashing algorithms, ensuring they cannot be read even in backups.
  • Audit logs and user profile information are accessible only to authorized personnel, and the full audit log is visible to moderators for security oversight.
  • In certain circumstances, such as legal obligations, we may be required to share data with law enforcement agencies.

5. Data Retention

  • HTTP request logs are automatically deleted after 2 weeks.
  • Usernames may be retained indefinitely for indexing forum topics and package identification.
  • Other personal data will be removed upon request unless retention is required by law or necessary for operational purposes.
  • Download tracking information (IP address caches) is deleted after 14 days.

6. Removal of Personal Information

If you wish to have your personal information removed, please submit a removal request. Note that if your username is linked to existing packages or forum topics, your account will be deactivated and wiped of personal details rather than being completely removed.

7. Future Changes

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Significant changes will be communicated via notices on the ScanShape website. Your continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
